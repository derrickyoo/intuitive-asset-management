Intuitive Asset Management, Inc.

Intuitive Asset Management is an IT consulting firm that focuses on high-end video editing and digital asset management solutions. Our friendly team of certified consultants provide high-quality integration, support, custom programming, and training for businesses and professionals who use Apple, Avid, Adobe, Autodesk, and many other media based technologies. We are committed to exceptional service and seek practical, reliable, cost-effective solutions to enable our clients to succeed.

Our specialty is in the deployment of high-end business and enterprise class technologies, which include Quantum StorNext, Apple’s Final Cut Pro, OS X Server, XSAN, and Final Cut Server. Final Cut Server is best suited for companies involved in the manipulation and distribution of video and audio content, such as post-production facilities, digital signage content creators, video-based training companies, audio facilities, medical facilities and many others. Our focuses is providing custom digital asset management solutions and automations for any video-centric environment. We design and implement the integration of Final Cut Server and customized digital asset management systems, ensuring that our DAM solution cooperates with all Mac, Linux and Windows systems that will meet your needs as we grow with you. We are the premier company in Los Angeles to assist in all of your Final Cut Server needs. From consultation and design, to implementation and ongoing support, we are prepared to work closely with all of our clients in making the transition to Final Cut Server as smooth as possible. What solution do you have handling your assets? Hopefully, IAM. Intuitive Asset Management means Smarter Assets! ™
